About Us

Welcome to LazyLister - the to-do list app that knows you probably won't get to it today, and that's okay! In a world full of productivity apps that make you feel like you're just not doing enough with your 24 hours, LazyLister stands out by embracing the art of doing...well, the bare minimum.

We understand that sometimes, the most ambitious thing you do in a day is make a list of all the ambitious things you're not going to do. And we're here for it. Why Choose Us?

Procrastinator-Friendly Features:

Our app is designed with the procrastinator in mind. Enjoy features like "Snooze for a Month," "Pretend I Didn’t See That Task," and "Delegate to Future Me."

Motivation, Schmotivation:

Who needs motivation when you have acceptance? Our notifications gently remind you that it's okay to not be okay with doing things. "Remember to not stress about not doing the thing today. Or tomorrow. No rush."

Achievement Badges for the Real World:

Earn badges not for completing tasks, but for the creative excuses you come up with for not doing them. "Dog Ate My To-Do List," "Was Too Busy Thinking About the Universe," and "Decided to Live in the Moment" are just a few fan favorites.

Our Philosophy:

At LazyLister, we believe that to-do lists shouldn’t be about doing more; they should be about stressing less. In a world obsessed with productivity, we offer a sanctuary for the serial procrastinators, the daydreamers, and the proudly lazy. After all, tomorrow is another day...probably.

Our Promise:

To always be there for you, ready to capture all the tasks you thought about doing and then wisely decided to put off. We're not just an app; we're a companion for those moments when ambition strikes, and you need somewhere to store it until the feeling passes. So, if you're looking to not get a lot done, you're in the right place. Let's embrace the joy of procrastination together. Because, in the grand scheme of things, isn't putting off till tomorrow what you could do today the ultimate form of optimism? Welcome to LazyLister - where tomorrow is the best time to do anything.
